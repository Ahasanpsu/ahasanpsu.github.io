Research Projects

May 2025 – Present · Indiana University, Guan Lab

Real-Time Quantification of RPA Amplicons via Single-Molecule Nanopore Sensing

  • Investigated RPA reaction kinetics using single-molecule nanopore sensing.
  • Performed qualitative and quantitative measurement of RPA amplicons by nanopore readout.
  • Developed experimental workflows for real-time amplification-reaction-coupled molecular counting.
  • Led assay optimization, nanopore measurements, real-time data analysis, and manuscript/proposal development.
Jun 2024 – May 2025 · Penn State & Indiana University, Guan Lab

Probe-Free Multiplex Detection of RPA Amplicons via Deep Learning

  • Developed a probe-free multiplex RPA assay for Mpox and RNase P, achieving 100% specificity.
  • Achieved 94.2% single-molecule classification accuracy using deep neural networks.
  • Detected signals from 75 bp to 500 bp DNA fragments using 3.4 to 12.4 nm pores.
  • Led nanopore experiments, signal-feature analysis, and deep learning model evaluation.
Feb 2024 – Dec 2024 · Penn State, Guan Lab

Multiplex Ligation-RPA for Early Prediction of Severe SARS-CoV-2

  • Developed a multiplex ligation-RPA assay with femtomolar sensitivity and 100% specificity.
  • Engineered a portable, sample-to-answer system integrating compact fluorescence detection and rapid miRNA extraction.
  • Validated the assay using 154 clinical saliva samples targeting miR-1273, miR-296, and miR-29.
  • Led assay optimization, clinical sample analysis, and portable workflow validation.
Aug 2022 – Feb 2024 · Penn State, Guan Lab

RPA-CRISPR Assisted Nanopore Sensing for Mpox Detection

  • Developed a CRISPR-Cas12a-RPA assay for Mpox detection with 100% specificity and an LOD of 16 copies/µL.
  • Designed a point-of-care-compatible electronic readout system as an alternative to optical detection.
  • Led CRISPR-RPA assay optimization, nanopore readout integration, and specificity testing.
Feb 2021 – Feb 2022 · Konkuk University, South Korea

Autonomous Droplet Concentration Gradient Generator

  • Designed an autonomous microfluidic oscillator to control pressures from 0.31 to 237 Pa.
  • Achieved dynamic concentration gradients over a 104-fold range using a DNA-mediated homogeneous FRET binding assay.
  • Led device design, droplet-generation experiments, and pressure-control characterization.
Feb 2020 – Feb 2021 · Konkuk University, South Korea

Autonomous Plasma Extraction from Whole Blood

  • Developed a passive plasma extraction device using constant-flow oscillation and droplet-generation principles for PCR-compatible sample preparation.
  • Contributed to device design, flow-control experiments, and plasma separation workflow development.

Translational Research & Innovation

  • Translated benchtop nucleic-acid assays into portable, sample-to-answer point-of-care systems integrating compact fluorescence detection and rapid nucleic-acid extraction.
  • Advanced a portable nanopore device toward real-time bio-assay detection with online positive/negative calling and multi-channel readout.
  • Built embedded readout and device integration on an NVIDIA Jetson Orin edge platform with Python-based APIs for real-time data acquisition and PC-to-hardware control.
